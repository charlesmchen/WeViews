Nesting WePanels

Here's a slightly more complicated example. The pillbox panel from the previous example (without the alignments) is now embedded in another WePanel with a WeImageView.
And here's the source:
The outer WePanel uses a "Vertical Fill" layout.
The footer panel with the buttons is not set to stretch, so it is laid out at its natural size which is just large enough to hold its contents.
The image view is set to stretch so that it receives any remaining space in its container.
