Install

  • Download the distribution from here (zip, tarball).
  • Add only the "WeViews" subdirectory to your project.
    Do not add the "WeViewsDemo" subdirectory to your project.
  • WeViews requires the iOS CoreText framework.
    In XCode, select your project, go to the "Build Phases" tab, and in the "Link Binary With Libraries" section, add a reference to CoreText.framework.
