Overview

The “natural grid” layout strategy.

If uniformGrid is true, all columns have width of widest column and all rows have height of tallest row.

Has either columnCount or rowCount. The other is calculated based on the number of items.

Any extra space from rounding errors is distributed evenly among the margins.

“Natural” size of the contents is based on the views in the layer.

See WePanelLayoutFillGrid, WePanelLayoutSpacingGrid.

Doesn’t perform well at scale.
