Overview

A WeSpacer is just an empty panel with stretch for filling empty space in “fill” layouts. The key difference is that “fill” layouts allocate extra space to WeSpacers with the LOWEST priority, ie. the WeSpacer will only stretch when all other “stretch” views are at their maximum size. This is especially useful for when you don’t want UIScrollViews to be larger than their content size.

See WePanel, WePanelLayout.
